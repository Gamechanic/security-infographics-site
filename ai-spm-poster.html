01
What AI-SPM is — posture management for the AI stack

AI Security Posture Management — continuous visibility and risk management across everything AI in your org: apps, models, agents, data, and the controls around them.

It takes the CSPM (cloud) and DSPM (data) idea — inventory the estate, find misconfigs & risky exposure, prioritize, fix — and points it at AI. You can't secure what you can't see.

A practice & an industry / vendor category, not a single spec — capabilities vary by product.

02
What it inventories — your AI attack surface, mapped

Every model, agent, dataset, prompt, embedding store, and tool permission — plus the controls around them — as one inventory.

03
AIBOM — AI Bill of Materials

The ingredients list for an AI system — what it's actually made of:

modelsdatasetspromptsembeddingstools / pluginsagents3rd-party AI deps

Like an SBOM, but for AI — the record AI-SPM builds and keeps current.

04
Agent & tool posture

For each agent, know its blast radius:

🤖
Where it lives — which apps & environments run it.
🔧
What it can call — which tools & plugins it's wired to.
📂
What it can read / do — data reach & actions it can take.

Least privilege — for agents.

05
Risks it surfaces — what good posture flags before an attacker finds it
👻
Shadow AI — unsanctioned tools & models nobody's tracking.
🗝️
Over-permissioned agents & tools — more reach than the job needs.
🌐
Exposed models / endpoints — open or unauthenticated inference.
📑
Sensitive data — in prompts, training sets, or vector stores.
🚧
Missing guardrails — no input / output filtering or limits.
📦
Model supply-chain risk — untrusted weights, deps, or sources.
06
The loop — discover → assess → prioritize → fix / monitor

Continuous, not one-off — the estate changes daily, so the loop runs forever.

09
Watch out
⚡
AI sprawls fast — every team ships an agent; the inventory is never "done".
🕳️
New leak paths — prompts & vector stores are data exfil routes classic tools miss.
🔑
Tool permissions are the new IAM — what an agent may call is now an access decision.
🏷️
"AI-SPM" is a market category — capabilities vary by vendor; check what a product actually covers.
10
The takeaway
AI-SPM is visibility first — inventory every model, agent, dataset, and prompt, then continuously assess and shrink the AI attack surface.
